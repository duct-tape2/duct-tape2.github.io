Before you send the repo

The package is deliberately narrow so price, delivery, and responsibility stay clear.

What is a good fit?

An existing CLI, template, browser tool, local-first utility, documentation pack, course asset, or small digital product with a specific buyer and one owner who can approve the result.

What is not included?

New product development, a custom commerce backend, payment-account setup, ongoing marketing, guaranteed traffic or sales, private credential handling, security or vulnerability work, crypto, spam, fake engagement, or unlicensed content.

Can you work with a private repo?

Ask by email first. Do not send credentials or private files through a public issue. Any necessary access is agreed separately after fit and payment-path confirmation.

What if checkout is unavailable?

Use the request or email path. I confirm a working checkout before payment and do not begin work until payment is confirmed.
