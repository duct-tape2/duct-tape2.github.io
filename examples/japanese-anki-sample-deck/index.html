Recognition card

PromptWhat does taberu mean?

Answerto eat

ContextA common everyday verb for meals, snacks, and casual conversation.

Production card

PromptSay "I want to eat ramen" in simple Japanese.

AnswerRamen o tabetai.

NoteThe -tai form expresses wanting to do an action.

Culture note

PromptWhen would you hear itadakimasu?

AnswerBefore eating.

NoteIt signals gratitude before a meal; it is not a direct one-word English match.
